Vikesh Mirchandani is a writer from Jakarta, Indonesia. His work has appeared in Porch Literary Magazine, Flash Phantoms, and FlashFlood Journal, and is forthcoming in the Edinburgh Anthology 2026. He won third place in the Anthology Flash Fiction Competition 2025 and has been longlisted for prizes by Flash 500 and Cranked Anvil Press.


He can be reached at vikesh.mirchandani@gmail.com.
